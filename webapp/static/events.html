📋 Irrigation & Rain Events

Upload a table of when it rained or was watered. You can also record how much the farmer would water, and why.
File format

Make a CSV (in Excel or Notepad) with these columns. The last two are optional:

date,type,amount,unit,farmer_would_water,farmer_reason
2026-06-08,rain,1.5,hours,,
2026-06-10,irrigation,30,minutes,20,soil looked dry
2026-06-13,irrigation,15,minutes,15,cloudy day
2026-06-15,rain,1.0,hours,0,rain expected

date = YYYY-MM-DD  ·  type = rain or irrigation  ·  amount = number (hours for rain, minutes for irrigation)  ·  farmer_would_water = minutes the farmer would water  ·  farmer_reason = why
